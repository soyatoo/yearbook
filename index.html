CC22 Guidelines

What is the yearbook?

A collection of pictures and stories that showcase the year that was, something for us all to look back on and remember exactly what 2022 felt like.

It's about seeing who was around and what we did, rather than amazing pics of people standing in tunnels (though a few of those do get in).

Deadline

Before 1st of Feb 2023.

Picture checklist

- Visibly exploring related;

- People in frame;

- Faces unblurred (they'll be blurred on request but I like to make them all the same style!);

- Taken between 1st of Jan 2022 and 31st Dec 2022;

- Photos you have personally taken! Or tell me who took it and I'll ask them if I can use it;

- Phone / DSLR / Film photos are all fine, so long as they're not tiny resolution;

Story checklist

- Exploring related;

- No names or location details if it's particularly spicy;

- If there are picture accompaniments to your story, send them in the place you think they'd best sit and we can workshop;

Where to send

By email: wasurechika@gmail.com
