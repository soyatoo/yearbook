CC22 Guidelines

Where to send

By email: wasurechika@gmail.com

Big files can be sent via Wetransfer

When to send

Before 1st of Feb 2023.

What should be in the pictures?

- Something visibly exploring related

- Images with people in them. Photos of landscapes / objects won't be accepted;

- However I will also accept pets / dolls / other weird shit if it's funny;

What to send

- Photos taken between 1st of Jan 2021 and 31st Dec 2021;

- Photos you have personally taken! Or tell me who took it and I'll ask them if I can use it;

- As many files as you like (some people send hundreds);

- Phone / DSLR / Film photos are all fine;

- Images larger than 3000px please, otherwise they don't print good!;

- The name you want credited (eg. Soy).

Want to send a story?

If you've got a good yarn to accompany an image, set of images, or even just a standalone one, send me an email. The only requirement is that it's a story from 2022, but it can be as short or as long as you like.
