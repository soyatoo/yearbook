CC21 Guidelines

Where to send

By email: wasurechika@gmail.com

Big files can be sent via Wetransfer

What to send

- Images with people in them. Photos of landscapes / objects / your cat won't be accepted;

- As many files as you like (some people send hundreds);

- Phone / DSLR / Film photos are all fine;

- Images larger than 3000px please, otherwise they don't print good!;

- I love to get stories that accompany your images;

- Photos taken between 1st of Jan 2021 and 31st Dec 2021;

- The name you want credited (eg. Soy).

When to send

Before 1st of Feb 2022.
